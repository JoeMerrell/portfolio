About

I'm an LA-based artist and web developer working in a variety of media including animation, video, sculpture, print, and scent. Born and raised in the pacific northwest, I earned a B.A. in philosophy and history at The Evergreen State College (Olympia, Washington) and an M.F.A. in film at CalArts (Valencia, California). Links to some of my recent projects are on the page below, and more details about my work history can be found at the CV link above. Please don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions!
